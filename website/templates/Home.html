A resume is a document that summarizes an individual's education, work experience, skills, and other relevant qualifications for a job application. It typically includes information about the applicant's employment history, education, professional accomplishments, and any relevant certifications or training. The purpose of a resume is to provide a concise and well-organized summary of an individual's qualifications, experience, and skills to potential employers, with the goal of securing an interview and ultimately a job offer. Resumes are typically customized for each job application and should be tailored to highlight the most relevant qualifications for the position.
